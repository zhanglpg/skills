# Note Template for Summarized Papers

Use this template when generating a paper/article note.

---

```markdown
# <Title>

> <Authors> · <Venue/Journal/Blog> · <Date>
> <URL>

## TL;DR
<1-2 sentence summary. What did they do and why does it matter?>

## Key Ideas
- <Core idea 1>
- <Core idea 2>
- <Core idea 3>
- ...

## What's Novel
<What does this contribute that didn't exist before? Be specific. Don't be generic.>

## Method / How It Works
<Brief explanation of the approach — enough to understand the mechanism, not a full replication guide>

## Results
<Key quantitative or qualitative results. What did they show?>

## Limitations & Open Questions
- <Limitation or gap 1>
- <Open question 1>
- ...

## Connections
<Link to related notes in the vault using [[wikilinks]]. e.g. "Relates to [[scaling laws]] and [[<The bitter lesson>]]. Contrasts with [[notes/Deepseek]]">

## Liping's Likely Take
<Based on Liping's interests and worldview — what would resonate, what would he push back on, what's the connection to his existing thinking?>

---
date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
status:: 📥
tags: #<tag1> #<tag2>
categories:: [[<tag1>]] - [[<tag2>]]
source:: <URL>
```

---

## Status Tags Reference

Match Liping's existing vault conventions:
- `📥` — just added/summarized, not yet deeply read
- `⌨️` — in progress / working notes
- `🌴` — evergreen / fully processed
